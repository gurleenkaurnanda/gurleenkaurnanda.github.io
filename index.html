Hello!

I am a fourth-year PhD Student in Mathematics at Louisiana State University under the supervision of Prof. Anton Zeitlin and Prof. Scott Baldridge. I received a B.S.-M.S. degree in Mathematics with a minor in Physics from IISER Bhopal in 2022.

I work in the intersection of representation theory, geometry, and physics. I am interested in the representation theory of supergroups, higher rank Thurston-Teichmüller theory, cluster algebras, and quiver varieties.

Geometric Satake Seminar

Welcome to the seminar page on Geometric Satake. Here you will find notes, references, and updates related to our study of the Geometric Satake equivalence.

Topics Covered:

  • Affine Grassmannians
  • Perverse sheaves and intersection cohomology
  • Tensor categories and Tannakian formalism
  • Langlands dual groups

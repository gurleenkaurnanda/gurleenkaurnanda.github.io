Geometric Satake Seminar

This is a dedicated subpage for the Geometric Satake seminar. The Geometric Satake equivalence is a deep result relating the geometry of the affine Grassmannian to the representation theory of complex reductive groups.

Here, I will share notes, talks, and related resources from seminar activities.

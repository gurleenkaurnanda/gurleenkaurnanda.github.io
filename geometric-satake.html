Geometric Satake

The Geometric Satake correspondence provides a bridge between geometric representation theory and the representation theory of complex reductive groups. It relates the category of perverse sheaves on the affine Grassmannian to representations of the Langlands dual group.

Introduction

At its core, the Geometric Satake equivalence recasts representation theory in geometric terms, allowing one to study algebraic objects through geometry. This has profound applications in the geometric Langlands program.

Applications

One of the key applications is in the geometric Langlands program, where the equivalence plays a central role in connecting categories of sheaves to automorphic forms and representations.
